About me

I am a student at Colégio Diocesano de São José (5ª), where I have been studying from kindergarten to the present. Alongside my regular schoolwork, I have been studying piano for the past 12 years and have passed the Grade 8 exam from ABRSM with merit.


I am also pursuing A-Level courses in Biology, Chemistry, and Mathematics, which have provided me with a deeper understanding of these subjects. Through these studies, I have developed a strong interest in the field of medicine, particularly in the areas of clinical practice and medical research.


My desire to save lives and solve complex medical problems is the primary driver behind my interest in the medical field. I believe the combination of my musical training, academic excellence, and growing passion for medicine has uniquely prepared me to make a meaningful contribution to this noble profession.


I am excited to continue exploring the various facets of medicine and to leverage my diverse experiences and skills to achieve my goals. I look forward to the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead.
